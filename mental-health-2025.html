Understanding Mental Health in 2025

Mental health awareness is growing. In 2025, the focus includes:

  • Reducing stigma through education
  • Accessible therapy via online platforms
  • Emphasis on self-care routines
  • Support for anxiety, depression, and trauma

Taking care of your mental health is just as important as your physical health.
