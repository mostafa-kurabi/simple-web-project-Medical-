Tips for a Healthy Heart

Your heart is the engine of your body. Keep it healthy by:

  • Eating low-fat, low-salt meals
  • Exercising at least 30 minutes daily
  • Avoiding tobacco and excess alcohol
  • Managing blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Getting regular medical checkups

Small steps make a big difference for heart health.
